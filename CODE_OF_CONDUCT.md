# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and respectful community.

## Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Prioritizing the project and community over ego

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or disruptive conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code.
